What is Typography
Typography is the art and technique of arranging
type to make written language

Legible, readable and appealing

Typography is the visual component of the
written word.

- Readability is one of the most important aspects of Web design
usability

Website readability is a measure of how easy it is for visitors to
read and understand online text

Readability depends on both a text's presentation and its
context

Poor readability scares readers away from the content
